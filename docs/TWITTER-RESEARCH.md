### What Works in Crypto Twitter (Observations)

**Format Patterns:**
1. **Thread hooks** - Start with something controversial or surprising
2. **Data-driven posts** - Numbers speak louder than hype
3. **Behind-the-scenes** - Show the decision-making process
4. **Meme integration** - Humor that resonates with degens
5. **Timely commentary** - React to market conditions in real-time

**Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Educational but entertaining
- Self-aware humor (we're called $FED after all)
- Diamond hands mentality without being cringe

**Engagement Hooks:**
- Questions to the community
- Predictions with rationale
- Hot takes that invite debate
- Celebrating milestones with holders

### Tweet Templates for Different Scenarios

**During Pumps (like now):**
- Focus on rewarding diamond hands
- Show distribution activity
- Celebrate without jinxing

**During Dips:**
- Emphasize fundamentals (real yield)
- Show accumulation activity (buybacks)
- Diamond hands messaging

**Neutral Markets:**
- Educational content about mechanics
- Stats and milestones
- Community building
